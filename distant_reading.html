Most Frequent Words

Through distant reading tools we aim at finding out the most frequent words and expressions (2 words, 3 words) in each of the 3 included subcorpora, a subcorpus being a sample of representative diary pages (prose) and poems of each author. Moreover we would like to perform the same type of analysis on the overall corpus so to highlight points of affinity and divergency.
